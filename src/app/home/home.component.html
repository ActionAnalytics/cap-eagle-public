About this site

The Public Review and Comment (PRC) website is part of B.C.'s digital products portfolio. The website has been launched as a pilot for the Province's Skeena Region, and will be continuously improved and rolled out provincially over the next year. We welcome feedback on the PRC website. To share your feedback, email use at nrtsprc@gov.bc.ca.

If you wish to view and comment on Crown Lands Applications in other regions of the Province, please visit arfd.gov.bc.ca
